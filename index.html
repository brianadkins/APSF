Dear Apex Community

After thoughtful discussion, the Board made the difficult and unanimous decision to close the Apex Public School Foundation.

Since our incorporation in 2018, APSF funded over $80,000 of teacher initiatives through the Teacher Grant Program and spotlighted over 300 extraordinary educators through a variety of recognition programs.

We are deeply grateful to all the donors, volunteers, educators, sponsors, partners, and community members who supported our mission over the years. This website serves as an archive to preserve the legacy of the grants and programs made possible through that support.

Our Mission

The Apex Public School Foundation was a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that maintained a fund to enhance the educational experience for students at Apex-area schools within the Wake County school district.

Incorporated in 2018, APSF supported evidence-based practices in education and instructional design across academics, the arts, technology, and professional enrichment.

Program Funding

Supporting initiatives not covered by district budgets or traditional funding sources.

Teacher Grants

Distributing educator grants for innovative programs, resources, and professional development.

Community Events

Hosting and funding community events and programs that brought schools and families together.

Public School Advocacy

Raising awareness and community support for public education in the Apex area.

Our Impact

Throughout our years of operation, the Apex Public School Foundation funded over $80,000 of teacher initiatives across Apex-area schools, supporting innovative classroom projects, technology, equipment, and programs that directly benefited thousands of students.

APSF also spotlighted over 300 extraordinary educators through recognition programs that celebrated the teachers and staff serving Apex-area WCPSS schools.

We are proud of the lasting impact these grants have had on our community's schools and students. You can explore the history of grants awarded on our Past Grants page.

Thank You

To our donors, sponsors, volunteers, and supporters: thank you for believing in public education and in the teachers who dedicate their lives to our children. Your contributions made a real difference in classrooms across our community.

To the educators who applied for and received grants: thank you for your creativity, dedication, and commitment to providing exceptional learning experiences for your students.
