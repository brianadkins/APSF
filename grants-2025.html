About Our Teacher Grants Program

Each year, the Apex Public School Foundation awarded grants ranging from $100 to $2,500 to support innovative and creative educational programs in Apex-area schools. Both individual teachers and teams could apply for funding to support projects not covered by traditional school budgets.

Grants supported a wide range of initiatives including classroom technology, educational resources, professional development, arts programs, STEM equipment, and creative learning experiences.
